Research

I'm studying how the nervous system makes our body move to design innovative solutions that help people recover lost skills. To do so: I track the spiking activity of large populations of motor units during controlled and natural behaviours (1); I develop neural interface-based training where participants progressively learn to modulate their motor unit activity in real time (2); and, ultimately, I teach them how to transfer these new skills to interact with computers or robotic limbs (3).
